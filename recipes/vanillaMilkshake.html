Vanilla Milkshake

Description

A chicken dish that is cooked on a rotisserie by using direct heat in which the chicken is placed next to the heat source.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Blend ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract together in a blender until smooth.
  3. Pour into glasses and serve.
